Walkers Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Walkers. We highly value and respect your personal privacy, and we are pleased to provide you with detailed information on how we access data, the methods of obtaining data, the specific purposes of data usage, and how we enhance your experience with Walkers.

1. How We Access Your Data and Visualize It

Walkers requests access to your device's built-in pedometer data. We only obtain this data with your consent to generate visual statistical charts and calculate your daily calorie consumption, walking distance, and walking time. We assure you that under no circumstances will we transmit your personal data to third parties. Your personal data is stored exclusively within the Walkers app on your device and is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

2. Permissions Used by Walkers

To ensure the proper functioning of Walkers and to enhance your service experience, Walkers requests the following permissions:

  • Activity Recognition Information Permission: Used to access your device's built-in pedometer data for generating visual statistical charts and calculating daily calorie consumption, walking distance, and walking time data.
  • Storage Permission (Applicable to devices running Android 9 and below): Walkers records your daily exercise data and provides a one-click sharing feature. This permission is used solely for temporary storage of user-shared content and does not permanently store or upload user data.

3. Enhancing Your Experience with Walker Services

We use the services outlined in Sections 3.1 and 3.2 of this privacy policy to help us understand how Walkers is used. We do not share any user data with anyone.

Usage Information

We collect activity information about your use of Walkers, such as app usage duration and app crashes.

Device Information

Hardware and software information, such as device ID, device model, app version, app crash information, and app name.

3.1 Firebase

Firebase is a cloud service provided by Google aimed at helping developers build high-quality applications and improve user experience. Walkers uses Firebase as an in-app monitoring solution to identify, monitor, and alert us to errors and vulnerabilities in the app. If the app encounters an application crash during normal use, crash information (such as crash time, device model, crash reason, and device operating system) will be sent to Firebase. For more information on Firebase's privacy policy, please visit Firebase Privacy Policy.

3.2 Email and Support

When you contact us via email, we may record personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, and email content to document issues and improve the Walkers app. We never use anyone's personal information (such as your name or email address) for any purposes such as promoting our apps or disclosing information for gain.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for general audiences aged 13 and above (16 and above for residents of the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 (or under the age of 16 in the European Economic Area). If you are under 13 years old (or 16 years old in the European Economic Area), please do not download or use our services.

5. Last Updated
